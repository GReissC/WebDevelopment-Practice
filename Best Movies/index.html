My Top 3 Movies

Top 3 best movies according to myself

Pixels

Very fun movie in the theme of 80s and 90s pop culture in a classic Adam Sandler style. 8/10

Coco

Beautiful movie depicting the mexican culture in a way that doesn't feel stereotypical. 9/10

The Nutty Professor (2008)

A more children-friendly and animated take on the story of "Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde", although it's pretty dated. 6/10 for normal people, 9/10 for me due to nostalgia.
